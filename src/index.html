The Sniper award

The Sniper award goes to the player with the highest prediction accuracy - most exact scores guessed correctly as a percentage of total predictions submitted. If the winner is already in the top 3, the award passes to the next most accurate player not already holding a prize.

🎯 The Sniper

🏅 Group prediction
Before June 10, vote on which player from the group will finish first and last. Votes are secret until the deadline. Winner bragging rights included.
🐂 Bold prediction badge
If you predict a result that nobody else dared to predict and you nail it, the admin can award you the legendary 🐂 badge. Wear it with pride.
🃏 Joker card
Once per tournament, you can activate a Joker on any match before deadline. If your prediction is correct, all points from that match are doubled. If wrong, you still lose nothing extra - the Joker just has no effect.
🎰 Double or Nothing
Once per week, pick any match and go Double or Nothing. Correct = your result and exact score points for that match are doubled. Wrong = those same points are deducted from your total. First scorer points are always safe either way.
🔥 Streak bonus
Get correct predictions in a row and earn a streak bonus! A streak of 3+ correct results earns +1 bonus point per match. A streak of 6+ earns +2 per match. Keep the momentum going!
🎯 Sniper of the Week
Each round, the player with the highest exact score accuracy earns the special "🎯 Sniper of the Week" badge on their profile. The badge rotates each round.
💬 Trash Talk Wall
Send short messages to the whole group - brag, taunt, or trash talk. Keep it fun and friendly! Messages are visible to all players.

Special game features 🎮
🃏 Joker Card
Once per tournament - tap the JOKER button on any match before the deadline. If your prediction is correct, all points for that match are doubled. You only get one!
🎰 Double or Nothing
Once per week, pick any match and go Double or Nothing. Correct = result and exact score points doubled. Wrong = those same points get deducted from your total. Your first scorer pick is always safe either way. No pressure. (Lots of pressure.)
